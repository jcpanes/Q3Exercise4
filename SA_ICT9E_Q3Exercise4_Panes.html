Caramoan


Caramoan is a peninsula on the eastern tip of Bicol. It's beaches are remote, clear, and breathtaking.

I went there on a business trip with my parents during 2017. My fondest memory during the trip was when me and my dad's colleague's played "agawan panyo" on the beach while island hopping. I remember enjoying it very much despite getting sand all over my body from falling on the ground while running. :D
